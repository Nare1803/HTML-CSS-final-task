About me

Curious about me? Here you have it:

I'm an enthusiastic designer and developer specializing in full stack development with React.js and Node.js. I am passionate about merging the technical and visual elements of digital products. Crafting exceptional user experiences, achieving pixel-perfect designs, and writing clean, efficient, and high-performance code are my top priorities.

I embarked on my web development journey in 2015. Over the past seven years, I have continually grown and evolved, embracing new challenges and mastering the latest technologies. Now, in my early thirties, I am dedicated to creating cutting-edge web applications using modern tools like Next.js, TypeScript, Nestjs, Tailwindcss, Supabase, and many more.

I am very much a progressive thinker and enjoy working on products end to end, from ideation all the way to development.

When I'm not deep in development, you can catch me exploring the vibrant world of Twitter or engaging with the inspiring community on Indie Hackers. I love following the journeys of early startups and indulging in some downtime. Follow me on Twitter for tech insights and my public building adventures, or check out my projects on GitHub.
